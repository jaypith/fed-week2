About Me

My name is Jasper Davis, currently I am employed as a graphic designer at a small digital signage company called Unoapp. At Unoapp I am one of the senior most graphic designers. I am taking this course in order to expand my skill set. At Unoapp we are doing more and more high end websites. Unoapp serves primarly the restaurant industry, and the demand for "modern" websites from restaurants owners is increasing faster and faster. Every restaurant owner, from the lowly "Mom & Pop" restaurants to the super high end lounges; they all want the parallax responsive high end restaurants. Giving them what they want and keeping up with the demand while remaining finacially viable is quite the challenge. I am looking for a way to deliver to them the type of website they want, while still remaining competitive in price & service.
